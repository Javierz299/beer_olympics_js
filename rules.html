Rules and Guidelines

If you are signed up to particpate in the games, you must compete in a minimum of 4 events. Failing to particpate at the time of the event. Will cost your country a gold medal (or next highest place if no gold).

Not all events need to be completed. Although the more events a country competes in will lead to more overall points.

If you and\or your entire team is signed up for an event and do not particpate. Your country will lose a medal one medal of the highest value.

Start times for events will be enforced. Olympians who are not at the event and/or not ready to go. A penalty will be given to that Olympian and country. **If one or more of your team members are not present and/or not ready to start the event at the given time. The Olympian who was not present at the given time may not enter after the start of the event. remaining Olympians may continue event**

If an Olympian signs up for an event and their are no other Olympians signed up for that event. The Olympian who signed up may attempt the event and take gold if no other Olympians are competing. **last minute entries: once the first Olympian has completed their event. They must call out for any last minute entries for that event. With a given time limit of 2 minutes for any late entires who would like to compete to take 1st place**
